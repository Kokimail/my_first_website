Meet the team members

Steve Kawakami, CPA

Steve is a highly experienced tax preparer and certified public accountant (CPA) with over 20 years of experience in the field. He has helped numerous clients, both individuals and businesses, with their tax preparation needs. Currently, he works as the CFO of B.Glen, a cosmetic company, where he oversees the company's financial operations and ensures compliance with tax laws and regulations.

Throughout his career, Steve has demonstrated his proficiency in tax preparation, staying up-to-date with the latest tax laws and regulations to provide accurate and reliable tax advice to his clients. He is known for his attention to detail and commitment to helping his clients minimize their tax liabilities while maximizing their tax benefits. With his extensive knowledge and experience, Steve is a valuable asset to any individual or business seeking tax preparation services.

Koki Kawakami, CPA

Koki is a highly experienced tax preparer and Certified Public Accountant (CPA) with an impressive track record in various accounting fields. He currently works as a Senior Financial Analyst and Program Manager for Amazon, where he is responsible for overseeing tech debt projects for accounting processes. Prior to joining Amazon, Koki worked as an in-charge senior associate at KPMG LLP, where he gained extensive experience in audit, financial reporting, and tax compliance for various clients in different industries such as consumer goods, financial services, energy, and biotechnology.

He has also worked as a Tax Accountant at J. Arthur Greenfield, a prestigious boutique tax firm, where he prepared complex tax returns for high profile individuals and small businesses. He ensured that clients receive the maximum benefit permitted under the tax law by extensively researching the best outcome based off of clients' circumstances.

Koki holds a Master of Accounting from University of Southern California and a Bachelor of Arts in Economics from University of California, Berkeley. With his track record of diverse experience, Koki is a reliable and trusted tax preparer who can assist individuals and businesses with their tax planning and compliance needs.
